Pancakes

Ingredients

  1. Flour
  2. Baking Powder
  3. Sugar
  4. Salt
  5. Milk and Butter
  6. Egg

Instructions

First you will want to sift all the dry ingredients together. Then mix all the wet ingredients in a different bowel. Mix well. Once the wet ingredients are mixed well. Dump them into the dry ingredients bowel and mix them together. After that scoop the batter onto a pan or griddle and let the cook for 2-3min then flip. Continue to cook till it is brown on both sides and then take off and add more batter.
