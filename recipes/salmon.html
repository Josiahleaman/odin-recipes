Salmon

Ingredients

  1. 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  2. 1 tablespoon dried basil
  3. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. 6 ounce fillets salmon
  5. 2 tablespoon butter
  6. 4 lemon wedges

Instructions

First stir all the dry ingredients together. Rub the mixture of garlic powder, dried basil, and salt on the salmon. Then melt the butter on a skillet over medium heat. Add the salmon and cook till brown and flakey. Finally eat it with the lemon wedges.
